About Me

Hello! I'm Azile Mbabane, a passionate photographer based in Cape Town. With over 4 years of experience in capturing life's most beautiful moments, I specialize in portrait, event, wedding, and brand photography.

My love for photography started when I was just a teenager, and it has grown into a lifelong passion. I believe that every photograph tells a story, and my goal is to capture those stories in the most authentic and artistic way possible.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring new places, trying out different cuisines, or reading up on the latest trends in photography.
